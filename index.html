About Me

I'm a clinical psychology and computer science student at the University of Oslo, with interests at the intersection of cognitive neuroscience and machine learning. I'm particularly interested in functions such as vision, perception, memory, and decision-making – in sum, how we experience and process the world around us – and the disruption of these processes by conditions like stroke, dementia, and various mental illnesses.
